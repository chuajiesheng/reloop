RELOOP: A Toolkit for Relational Convex Optimization¶



Welcome to reloop! This is the web home of the Relational Optimization project of the Data Mining group at TU-Dortmund. Here you will find software and theory produced in our research efforts.

From here you can:

  • get an overall picture of what RELOOP is about by checking out the introduction;
  • get started with our code – head over to download, installation and then our Quickstart tutorial;
  • navigate the complete code decumentation;
  • find a list of all related papers on our bibliography page.
